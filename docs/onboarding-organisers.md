# Onboarding New Organisers

When someone joins the organising team, we get them set up with access to our
tools and make sure they feel welcome and oriented. This page is the checklist
for onboarding a new organiser.

## Access and tools

- **Discord** — give them the "organiser" role on our Discord server.
- **Google Drive** — share the organisers' Google Drive with them.
- **Notion** — give them full guest access.
- **Role-specific tools** — give them access to any other tools needed for the
  particular role they're taking on.

## Welcome and orientation

- Send a personalised welcome message on the organisers' general channel on
  Discord.
- Send shorter orientation messages on any other relevant channels, introducing
  them to the people and processes involved in their role.
